Data Deletion Instructions

To delete your MapLeap data:

  1. In-app (coming soon): Go to Settings → Delete Account to request deletion.
  2. By email: Write to alaka.softwares@gmail.com from your account email. We will delete your data from Firebase within 7 days.
  3. Facebook data: For Facebook sign-in, you can also manage data directly via your Facebook account settings.

For details on data collection and use, please see our Privacy Policy.
